Yes, Indian citizens planning to visit Sri Lanka for tourism, transit, or business purposes must apply for an e-visa, also known as Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA), before their trip.

-   **Sri Lanka Transit Visa Application:** Required for Indians transiting through Sri Lanka for up to 2 days. It is valid for a single entry and for 6 months from the date of approval.
