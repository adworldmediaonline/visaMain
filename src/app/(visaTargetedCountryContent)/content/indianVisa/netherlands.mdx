### Critical Prerequisites for Dutch Travellers:

- **Passport Validity:** Your passport must be in effect for a minimum of six months after your scheduled arrival in India, and it must contain at least two empty pages.
- **Photograph:** A recent photograph in passport size that satisfies the eVisa prerequisites is required.
- **Flight Itinerary:** Confirmed round-trip ticket to India, including points of entry and departure.
- **Information Regarding Lodging:** Hotel reservations or a letter of invitation from your host in India.
- **Financial Proof:** Documents from a financial institution or one's bank account that demonstrate adequate funds to cover expenses for the duration of the stay.
