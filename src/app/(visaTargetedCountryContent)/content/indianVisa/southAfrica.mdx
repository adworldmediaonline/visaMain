### What You Need to Know About the Indian e-Visa:

**Who can apply:**

- People from South Africa can apply for an e-Visa to India.
- People who want to travel should apply for an e-Visa at least 4 days before they plan to leave.
- There is no room for foreign passports; only regular South African passports are accepted.

#### Required Documents for Visa Applications

The documentation required to apply for an Indian visa varies according to the class of visa. However, frequently required documents include:

- Passport: A valid Kenyan passport with two vacant pages for stamps, which must be six months or longer from your point of arrival in India.
- Photograph: A recent passport-sized image that meets the requirements for Indian visa photos.
- Application Form: An appropriately filled out and signed request for a visa.
- Flight Itinerary: A validated round-trip itinerary or ticket to India that comprises both the arrival and departure points.
- Supporting Documentation: Reservations for hotels or an extended letter of invitation from an Indian host.
- Financial Verification: Bank statements or other pertinent financial documentation that substantiates the traveller's sufficient funds to cover their expenses throughout their stay in India.

##### Classification of eVisas for South African Travellers

**Tourist Visa**

Use a Tourist eVisa to explore the splendour and variety of India. Tourism eVisas are available in three varieties for South Africans, depending upon the purpose of their journey:

- The 30-day double-entry eVisa: Well-suited for brief visits as it grants permission for two entries during its validity period.
- The 1-Year Multiple-Entry eVisa: Provides travellers with the opportunity to visit India at various points throughout the year, while restricting each stay to a maximum of 90 days.
- 5-Year Multiple-Entry eVisa: This visa grants frequent visitors to India the ability to enter the country for a maximum of five years, with each entry restricted to a duration of 90 days.

**Business Visa**

Business travellers from South Africa are eligible for the Indian eBusiness Visa. Permitting stays of up to 180 days per visit, facilitates business expansion, partnerships, and meetings in India. It is valid for one year.

**Medical Visa**

Medical treatment in India is permitted for South African citizens with an eMedical Visa. This document guarantees unrestricted access to medical care for patients and their attendants by permitting triple entry and is valid for sixty days.
