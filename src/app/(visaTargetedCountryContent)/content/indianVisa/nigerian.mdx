###### Critical Prerequisites for Nigerian Tourists

- **Passport Validity:** Verify that your passport is valid for a minimum of six months after your scheduled arrival in India, and that it contains at least two vacant pages.
- **Passport-Sized Photo:** A recent photo in passport size that satisfies the e-Visa prerequisites.
- **Flight Itinerary:** A confirmation itinerary is a round-trip ticket that specifies the point of entry and departure from India.
- **Lodging Documentation:** Documentation Relating to Lodging: Hotel reservation confirmations or letters of invitation from hosts in India.
- **Financial Proof:** Bank statements or other financial documentation demonstrating that you possess adequate funds to cover the expenses of your stay.
