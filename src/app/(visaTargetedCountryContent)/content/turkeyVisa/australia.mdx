## FAQs

### Answers to common questions related to Turkey visas

### Qn1: What is the validity of a Turkey Visa?

Turkey tourist visas allow for numerous entries, but each entry can last up to three months. The visas themselves are valid for 12 months from the date of issuance.

Qn 2: Can I extend my Turkey visa while I'm in Turkey?

You may ask for a visa extension, however, you have to do it before the one that is present expires and fulfil all the conditions.

Qn 3: I'm passing through Turkey; do I require a visa?

If you are planning on spending more than eight hours in Turkey or leaving the transit lounge, you must obtain a transit visa.

Qn 4: Does one need to get vaccinations to enter Turkey?

Although usually, you do not require the vaccine to be able to enter Turkey, the best thing is to check for the recent health warnings when leaving.

Qn 5: Can I work in Turkey on a tourist visa?

No, you cannot switch from a tourist to a work visa adventure once you are in Turkey. A work visa must be applied for from your nation.

Qn 6: What happens if I overstay my Turkey visa?

Overstaying a visa can have legal consequences such as being imprisoned, deported, and forbidden entry to Turkey
