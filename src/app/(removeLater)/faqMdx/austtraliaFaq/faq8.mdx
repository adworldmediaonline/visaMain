**Visa Requirements**: Australian citizens need a valid visa to enter India, regardless of the purpose of the trip.

**Application Process**: The visa application can be made online through the [official portal](#). The process involves uploading a photo and passport page, paying the eVisa fee online, and receiving the Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) via email.

**Types of Indian Visa**: There are several types of e-Visas available for Australian citizens:

- **e-Tourist Visa:** Valid for 30 days from the date of arrival in India, single-entry, and cannot be extended.

- **India e-Business Visa:** Valid for 365 days, multiple-entry, each entry shall not exceed 180 days.

- **India e-Conference Visa:** Valid for 30 days, single-entry only.

**Entry to the Country**: On arrival in India, present the printed ETA at the Immigration Check Post where the eVisa will be stamped on the passport.

**Other Basic Requirements**: All foreign nationals entering India are required to possess a valid international travel document in the form of a national passport with a valid visa from an Indian Mission/Post or eVisa (Limited Categories) from Bureau of Immigration, Ministry of Home Affairs.
