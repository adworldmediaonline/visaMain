Learning a few greetings and essential phrases can be helpful and show respect for the local language. Many online resources and apps can help.

Bonus tips for Canadian Citizens Travelling India:

Bargaining: It's common in markets and bazaars. Be polite and respectful, and enjoy the experience!

Air quality: Consider wearing a mask in highly polluted cities like Delhi.

Food & water: Stick to bottled water, cooked food, and reputable restaurants to avoid stomach upsets.

Electricity: Plugs are typically different, so pack a universal adapter.

Note: These are just general tips. Research your specific destination from multiple sources before jumping on a plane and consult official sources in your home country and in the visiting country for the latest information before you start your trip to India.
