### What Is Indonesia eVisa?

The Indonesia eVisa allows eligible travellers to enter the country for up to 30 days for tourism, business, government visit, goods purchasing, or transit purposes. You can apply for an e-Visa online through the official website of the Directorate General of Immigration of Indonesia.

### Who is eligible to apply for an Indonesia visa online?

You are eligible to apply for an Indonesia visa online if you hold a passport of one of the 75 countries that are listed on the official website of the Directorate General of Immigration of Indonesia. You also need to have a valid email address, a credit or debit card, and a travel itinerary that shows your purpose and duration of stay in Indonesia.

As of now, the Indonesia Visa is available to residents of several countries including:

Australia | Brazil | Canada | China | France | Germany | India | Japan | Russia | South Korea | United Kingdom | United States of America.

Note: If your country of residence is not listed, or if you plan to stay in Indonesia for a longer duration, you will need to apply for a visa through the traditional offline process at the Indonesian embassy or consulate in your jurisdiction.

#### What is the cost of Indonesia VISA?

The cost of an Indonesia eVisa varies based on your nationality and the type of visa you are applying for. For instance, the Indonesia Visa on Arrival costs around IDR 500,000 (approximately USD 32.98). However, the cost for US citizens is approximately USD 974.

So, it is advisable to check the official Indonesia eVisa website for the most up-to-date information on fees and payment methods.

### What is the processing time for an Indonesian eVISA?

The processing time for an Indonesia eVISA depends on the type of visa and individual circumstances. On average, the following processing times can be expected:

-   Tourist eVISA to Indonesia: 3 to 7 business days
-   Business eVISA to Indonesia: 5 to 10 business days
-   Social eVISA to Indonesia: 3 to 7 business days
-   Cultural eVISA to Indonesia: 5 to 10 business days

#### How to Apply for Indonesia eVISA Online?

Applying for an Indonesia eVISA is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to embark on your Indonesian adventure:

1. Begin Your Journey: Visit the official Indonesia eVISA website or use the user-friendly app. Fill in your passport details, travel plans, and choose your preferred payment method (debit, credit, or PayPal). Click "Apply" and receive a confirmation email with further instructions.

3. eVISA Approval (if required): Some applicants may be asked to attend an appointment at the nearest Indonesian embassy or consulate. A comprehensive guide will be provided, ensuring a hassle-free process.

##### Where can I enter and exit Indonesia with an e-Visa?

You can enter and exit Indonesia with an e-Visa through any of the 29 designated airports and 88 designated seaports that are listed on the official website of the Directorate General of Immigration of Indonesia. You cannot use an e-Visa to enter or exit Indonesia through land borders or other ports of entry or exit that are not designated for e-Visa holders.
