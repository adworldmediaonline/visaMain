### Your Indian eVisa Options

Depending on your purpose of visit and nationality, you can choose from different **eVisa types** for India. Here are some of the most popular eVisa types:

## E-Tourist Visa:

This e Tourist Visa is for those who want to visit India for sightseeing, recreation, cultural or spiritual activities, visiting friends or relatives, etc. You can apply for a single entry or multiple entry e-Tourist Visa for 30 days, 1 year, or 5 years.

## E-Business Visa:

This e-Visa is for those who want to visit India for business-related activities such as trade, commerce, meetings, conferences, exhibitions, etc. You can apply for a multiple entry e-Business Visa for 365 days.

## E-Medical Visa:

This e-Visa is for those who want to visit India for medical treatment or consultation at recognized hospitals or medical institutions. You can apply for a triple entry e-Medical Visa for 60 days.

## E-Medical Attendant Visa:

This e-Visa is for those who want to accompany a patient who is traveling to India for medical treatment or consultation. You can apply for a triple entry e-Medical Attendant Visa for 60 days.

There are also other e-Visa types such as e-Conference Visa, e-Journalist Visa, e-Research Visa, etc. You can find more details about them on our website.

#### So, Who Holds the Key?

Based on **"India’s online visa services"**, here’s the following eligibility criteria:

-   **Eligibility Criteria - #1:** You must be a citizen of one of the 160+ countries that are eligible for online visa services.

-   **Eligibility Criteria - #2:** You must have a passport with at least 6 months validity from the date of arrival in India and at least two blank pages for stamping.

-   **Eligibility Criteria - #3:** You must not have a Pakistani passport or Pakistani origin. If you do, you need to apply for a regular visa at the Indian mission.

-   **Eligibility Criteria - #4:** You must not have a diplomatic or official passport or a laissez-passer travel document. If you do, you need to apply for a regular visa at the Indian mission.
